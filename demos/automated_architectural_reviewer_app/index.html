Method

How this first version reviews an architecture.

1. Detect

Looks for architecture signals such as identity, network, data, monitoring, backup, CI/CD, cost controls, AI workflow and human review.

2. Score

Applies a transparent rule set and weights findings as red, yellow, or green depending on likely risk and missing evidence.

3. Recommend

Generates practical next steps, operational questions, and a 30-day roadmap that can start a focused architecture conversation.

This is not a replacement for a senior architecture review. It is a fast structured first pass designed to expose risks, missing questions, and next steps.
